3D Modelling and Animation

This is my first foray into using Maya. I wanted to explore the capabilities of the software's key frame animation, image/video mapping, and stereo camera technologies. What you see here is the result of various experimentations with creating a scene and traversing it with a camera. Technology used: Maya, Ableton, Adobe After Effects.

Skavengr

This project lets you organize and invite people on scavenger hunts anywhere in the world. It uses google maps to place markers that the quest creator can then write a clue for and specify a time window for the quest to take place. Participants use the app on their phones to track when they discover a location and receive a notification upon completion. The first person to finish all the clues gets a virtual trophy! Built with Ruby on Rails with Carl Reiner and Joe Rehfuss.
